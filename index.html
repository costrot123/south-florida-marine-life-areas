Explore South Florida's Marine Ecosystems and Their Locations!

Florida is home to a remarkable amount of incredible and complex ecosystems. This map is designed to bring light to a few of Florida’s many fascinating marine ecosystems. Click on the Map above and interact with the marked locations. When you are ready to explore an area in more depth, click on “Explore,” to begin discovering the location’s marine ecosystem. For the best experience, begin exploring the Key West area first.
